Type of Kitchen Module

A modular kitchen is definitely a thing of beauty. With a perfect blend of aesthetics and functionality, a well designed modular kitchen has plenty of storage space and makes cooking so much convenient, comfortable and easy. The modules come in unlimited options to suit your space, needs, family size and more.

Wall Module

  • With shutter
  • Open storage
  • Dish drainer
  • Lift Up

Elegant wall fittings with seamless shutters make a perfect niche for your treasued crockery and other dishes used on special occassions.

Flaunt your kitchen in style. The open storage can be used for regulary used objects such as napkin holder, spoons and knives holder, spices and condiments container etc. Alternatively, you can can use the open space to create a green ambience in your kitchen by decorating small plated table pots. Even showpieces can be decorated in the open space to give the kitchen the desired facelift

Dish drainer is an extremely useful and innovative aspect of your kitchen. Depending on the weather, the washed dishes sometimes take a lot of time to dry up and storing damp dishes in closed compartments can be an unhealthy option. This dish drainer can be used for arranging dishes after cleaning. Once the dishes are completely dry, those can be placed in the respective cabinets.

Lift up doors to wall fitting cabinets can make the kitchen space less congested. During busy parties and gatherings at home when there are multiple people using the kitchen, these lift up door cabins can make working in the kitchen easier. With a robust pully system, these lift up doors can make adjusting bulkier dishes easy as one doesn't need to hold the door in one hand and the heavy dish in another hand. This makes life so much easier.

Tall Unit

  • Shutter
  • Built in microwave

The shutter in the tall unit is a very smart option. It helps compartmentalize all the similar items together inside one grand spacious unit. Easily accessible and easy to clean, the shutter in tall unit can be a real saviour for your kitchen storage woes.

Built-in microvawe gives a trendy look to your kitchen space. Easy to manage and maintain, a built-in microvawe is a hassle free option.

Island Counter

Island counter can be your innovative space. You can go as creative as you want while decorating or setting up the island counter. The island counter is where you will laze around in the kitchen. It can reflect the liveliness of the kitchen and you can also use it for placing handy items.

Shelves

These shelves give a subtle countryside essence to your kitchen. Wooden planks can give a rugged yet stylish look to the kitchen. The shelves can be extremely useful in arranging miscellaneous yet handy items. The items will not get cluttered here and there and can be easily found.
