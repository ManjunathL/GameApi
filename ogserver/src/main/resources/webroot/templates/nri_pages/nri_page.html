FAQs

Do I need to visit from time to time? How will I know if the quality of the materials used is good? There will be a host of questions on your mind. We've answered a few below. If you have more, we're just a phone call or email away.

Q

How do I make an account on mygubbi ?

Dear Buyer! It’s very easy to register an account on mygubbi. Just click on the “Log In” link on the top right hand side of the page. If you are already a member, fill in your account details and sign in. A new member can make an account instantly by clicking on “Register” link. A new window will open, fill in your basic details and choose a highly-secure password. Simple! You are a member of mygubbi now. Sign in again with your account details.

Q

What are the advantages of having a mygubbi account ?

mygubbi account comes up with manifold benefits apart from ease of shopping. You will be getting updates on discounts or seasonal offers running on our store. We would love to share our exclusive collection and updates with you. Do go through our newsletters and stay connected.

Q

What are the payment terms for shopping at mygubbi ?

For standard products, we expect 50% of the amount to start manufacturing process. Rest can be paid once the products are finished and packaged in our warehouse for delivery. For customized orders, we follow a 10-40-50 payment policy. We expect 10% of initial budget as advance which will be adjusted against the total cost of the project later. The next 40% will be expected on finalization of the design and the last 50% will be paid once material is ready for shipment.

Q

Do you include service tax in the cost price ?

Yes, the cost price is cumulative of manufacturing and designing cost along with additional pricing (for extra services), VAT and service tax.

Q

Why don’t you offer “Cash on Delivery” option to buyers ?

All the products are “Customized” items. They are designed and manufactured especially for an individual. Craftsmen and artisans require an advance payment before they start manufacturing any particular item. Hence, we don’t offer “Cash on Delivery” option.

Q

How should I handover the house keys?

There are multiple options to hand over the keys.
Option 1 - The client can hand over the keys to the designer at the time of final discussion.
Option 2 - Clients can leave the keys at the Experience Center.
Option 3 - Clients can also come over to the site on the day of delivery with keys, in order to have the touch and feel of the products.
Option 4 - In case the client is, for various reasons, unable to drop the keys, then MyGubbi team will go to the clients place to collect it.

Q

What if I don’t like the product ? Can I return it ?

We assure you that one will definitely adore the products designed after much care and intricacy. Our designers and artisans strive to create the finest specimen to refurbish your abode. We heartily apologize as we don’t receive any returns. mygubbi products are designed after customer’s inputs, opinions and regular audits. After putting immense efforts our designers are able to craft the perfect specimen for you. We haven’t received any complaints from our buyers so far. So, don’t worry!

Q

Do I have to pay for shipping of the products ?

No, we don’t charge any shipping or delivery charges. Our delivery services are absolutely free.

Q

How can I contact designer if I want changes ?

mygubbi offers you the benefit of modifying the design before manufacturing commences. You can twitch the design whenever you want. You can reach us at: 08088 860 860 or drop a mail at reach@mygubbi.com

Q

How can I track my order ?

You will get a confirmation mail post manufacturing of the project. You can contact us on customer care number to track the shipment. If you want to visit the design studio to know the status of item, you are free to pay us a visit anytime.

Q

Do you deliver in my city ?

Please write to us to check if we deliver in your city. You can simply fill the enquiry form with your correct contact details. We will get in touch with complete details.

Q

Why are mygubbi products not available at my pin code ?

The delivery of the products is restricted by many factors- may be legal issues or state regulations. We can swiftly deliver the product in Bangalore or nearby location. We want to make sure that we are able to deliver and install the products with safety and warranty. Hence, we don’t deliver at certain locations. But we are expanding on daily basis, do keep an eye on us.
