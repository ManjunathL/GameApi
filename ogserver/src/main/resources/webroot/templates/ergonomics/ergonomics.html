Ergonomics

A smartly designed kitchen with careful planning offers the best ergonomics for comfort, efficiency and productivity. By teaming ergonomically designed fixtures and appliances with your kitchen layout, working in your kitchen will always be a pleasurable experience. Ergonomics in a kitchen is supported by the work triangle, which is commonly known as the “working space”. Your kitchen design and layout largely impacts the work triangle. With a layout that offers great ergonomics, your culinary experience can always be a pleasurable experience. Ergonomics in kitchen offers you the ease of access to appliances, fixtures and other essentials when moving around in the kitchen. Installing the right drawers, cabinets and storage solutions based on the layout of your kitchen is the key to achieving good kitchen ergonomics.

A modular kitchen is all about style and ease of access. When equipped with the right accessories and hardware, your kitchen boasts an aesthetic appeal that offers style and functionality. A fully functional and ergonomically designed kitchen offers a better interface with appliances and fixtures for a pleasurable culinary experience. The prime objective of ergonomics with modular kitchen is to ensure a flexible working space that offers you a comfortable experience when working in the kitchen. A modular kitchen with the right ergonomics ensures that you interact with your kitchen unit in a way that offers functionality and makes you more efficient.

Both physical and cognitive ergonomics are equally important to ensure a smooth workflow in your kitchen. A good work triangle ensures that you move comfortably between the sink, refrigerator and stove. This also means that there are no obstructions like an island or cabinets in the work triangle to ensure your kitchen features the best ergonomics for a clutter free kitchen workspace.
