Material

  • MDF
  • PLY
  • PLPB

Medium Density Fiberboard is made by processing softwood and hardwood residuals. It is engineered to be resistant to termite and borer. MDF is sturdy and durable material with a smooth surface that is ideal to be used for lamination while furnishing interiors. It is easy to cut, carve and hence makes it easy for detailed designing. Though MDF has similar features of a particle board, but is much denser and strong. It is cheaper compared to plywood.

Plywood is made by bonding many layers of softwood or hardwood or both with adhesive. It features a smooth finish by accommodating dyes and paint better. It is factory manufactured and has a cross lamination of finish with veneers, which ensures that they do not expand, shrink or wrap when exposed to high temperature, moisture and water. Plywood is resistant to chemicals and is no-corrosive. Perfect for insulation, plywood can be used to control the temperature and reduce noice pollution.

Pre-Laminated Particle Board is made by compressing glue and wood fibres under high pressure and heat. The board is then given a smooth finish with laminates or veneers. PLPB is an ideal furnish material to consider if you are looking for a wide range of options that give sophisticated and premium finish. It is economical, resistant to moisture, termiteand borer. The material is recommended to be used for wardrobes and shelves.

FINISH MATERIAL

  • LAMINATE
  • PVC FOILS
  • PU PAINT
  • ACRYLIC

Made from thin slices of wood or synthetic materials, laminates feature the looks of wood grains and is cost-effective when compared with veneer or solid wood. This layer of finish is usually bonded to particle board or MDF wood and is processed by applying high pressure and heat. This helps furniture to be resistant from water, moisture, heat, humidity and also is also easy to clean and maintain.

PU paint is a clear coating that is available in both water-base and oil-base. It features variety of finishes including gloss finish and matte. This paint is easy to apply on any surface and provides a premium finish. PU paint offers you with other advantages such as UV protection, weather resistance, crack prevention and improved durability.

PVC foil membrane is a thermoplastic material. It is easy to cut and can be applied on other wooden material to get a premium finish. PVC foil is widely recommended to be used on kitchen cabinets as it attracts less oil particles and can be easily cleaned. These foils are usually used as an alternative to wood for its realistic wood-like finish. They are available in a wide range of finish designs and colours.

Acrylic based paint is a non-toxic coating. It has a resin based finish and is water soluble, however, is water resistant when dry. Resistant to heat and scratch, this coating paint is ideal to be applied on any material that is mostly exposed outdoors. Cleaning an acrylic finish material is simple and easy to maintain.
