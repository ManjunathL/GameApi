Materials

The material used can be rightly called the essence of your home. No matter how attractive the design or how sophisticated the interiors, it is the material that brings out the true strength and beauty.

MDF

Medium Density Fiberboard or MDF is an engineering marvel and renders strength to the design. Extremely dense and durable, MDF can complement innovative designs and structures.

PLY

An extremely popular choice, Ply is highly versatile and can be chosen depending on the strength, durability and designing required. Ply naturally complements polishing surfaces.

PLPB

Pre-Laminated Particle Board lends life to the design and also looks best on modern structures. The best part about PLBP is that it is moisture resistant, durable and also lightweight.
