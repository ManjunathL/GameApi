Appliance

Appliances form the very backbone of your kitchen. From trendy hobs and chimneys to sleek built-in microwaves, ovens and dishwashers, these appliances make your life easy and relaxed. Comfort coupled with luxury, the appliances can actually transform the look and feel of your kitchen.

HOB

  • Steel
  • Glass
  • Induction

Easy to manage and easy to maintain, steel hobs can make your cooking experience completely pleasurable. Available in various sizes and designs in terms of burner placement, steel hobs are also durable. These hobs are extremely safe and can complement almost all colours of kitchen cabinets.

Smooth and shiny glass hobs are an absolute delight. With proper heat distribution and option for customized burner placement, these glass hobs can lend elegance to your kitchen. Available in attractive patterns and designs, glass hob can very much be the highlight of your kitchen. Utility and beauty both can be achieved with glass hob.

Faster, smoother, safer, and more efficient, induction hob uses magnetic induction instead of the regular flame. Heating as well as cooking is quick in induction. This comes with some extra features too such as timers, temperatures, child safety locks. Impress your guests and make your cooking experience pleasurable with this expert hob.

Chimney

  • Fixed on wall
  • Built In

If you like to go the usual way, then fixed on wall chimney is the best option for you. Keeps your kitchen fume free and stuffiness free with this state-of-the-art chimney.This is designed for those traditional kitchens where the stove is near the wall. As this is fixed on the wall, there is less or no ducts required for the exhaust smoke. The chimney can be available in various colours to match the theme of your kitchen.

This is the most comfortable option and makes kitchen maintenance so much easier. If you feel a chimney will affect the beauty of your kitchen, then this is the best option. It is directly fitted below the cabinet unit and the extra parts are not seen.

Built In Appliance

  • Microwave
  • Dish washer
  • Oven

Pretty much a lifeline in today's time, an advanced microwave with enabling functions can be a real time saver as well as redefine convenience. Sleek and sophisticated in design it can add to the vibe of the kitchen. Extremely safe, easy to maintain and most importantly easy to clean, microwave can be incredibly useful for you.

Don't let your precious crockery be washed ordinarily and be prone to breakage. A seamless dishwasher can be the perfect answer to this hassle. Easy to operate and convenient to manage, dishwasher is more about enhanced lifestyle and modern ways of living.

Bake some sweet goodness in your kitchen and let the aroma travel across your home. Beautifully fitted, expert functional oven can be your happy place at home. With its incredibly convenient placement and easy to learn functions, you can give a try at baking by trying new recipes.

SINK

  • Single Bowl
  • Double
  • Composite Material

A hygienic and perfectly designed sink to keep your unclean utensils. With enough capacity and spacious surface, this sink can accommodate large number of utensils. So if you have a party planned, don't worry about arranging unwashed dishes.

This one makes life so much easier by compartmentalizing utensils. Keep your breakable utensils in a different sink and daily use utensils in the other one.

If you thought your sink can't add to the beauty of your kitchen, you were wrong. Composite material sink is innovative and lends a unique tone to the kitchen. Who said sink can't be a thing of beauty?
