About Us

MyGubbi is a consumer tech company, enabling customers to design their dream home. We offer interior design solutions and home merchandise across various categories that are perfect to style modern homes. With the help of our experienced designers, your dream home is just a click away. Choose from a range of sophisticated designs with 3D renders to get your home the right look you desire. We create beautiful homes with you to help you reflect your lifestyle to the home decor in the best possible way.

‘Home is where the heart is’ said Pliny the Elder, a Roman philosopher. Today, almost two thousand years later, that single thought has come to define everything we stand for.
