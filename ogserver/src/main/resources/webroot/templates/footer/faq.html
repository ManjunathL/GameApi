Most Common FAQs

What are the payment terms for shopping at mygubbi?

For standard products, we expect 50% of the amount to start the manufacturing process. Rest of the amount can be paid once the products are finished and packaged in our warehouse for delivery.
For customized orders, we follow a simple 10-40-50 payment policy. We expect 10% of initial budget as advance which will be adjusted against the total cost of the project later. The next 40% will be expected on finalization of the design and the last 50% will be paid once material is ready for shipment.

Do you include service tax in the cost price?

Yes, the cost price is cumulative of manufacturing and designing cost along with additional pricing (for extra services), VAT and service tax. There are no hidden charges or fee for work delivered.

Why don’t you offer “Cash on Delivery” option to buyers?

All the products are “Personalized” as per buyer’s choice. They are designed and manufactured especially for an individual. Craftsmen and artisans require an advance payment before they start working on any particular project. Hence, we don’t offer “Cash on Delivery” option.
