Meet the TEAM

mygubbi founding team comprises of seasoned entrepreneurs with proven track record, who have incubated and developed multiple businesses earlier. The team has cumulative experience of more than 100 years and has worked in senior positions in large scale businesses like Wipro, Infosys, Tesco, Future Group, Triology, Tata Coffee, Sobha Interiors,etc. The team has good expertise in technology, design operations and retail businesses.

Umesh Sangurmath

Founder And Director
B.E. from NITK, Surathkal

Umesh Sangurmath is an accomplished leader with a proven track record in incubating and scaling large businesses. Prior to founding mygubbi, Umesh handled the prestigious position of CIO with Future Group and also worked at the senior positions at Wipro Technologies. He co-founded and successfully exited ‘Smart Retail’, a chain of food and grocery in India. With more than two decades of experience, he has expertise in Retail operations, Enterprise IT systems and has demonstrated leadership with leading corporates of large size.
